Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how IPTV Player ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

IPTV Player is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not use cookies or similar technologies
  • We do not collect usage statistics
  • We do not serve advertisements

Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following data only on your device (locally):

  • Playlist URLs: The M3U/M3U8 playlist URLs you add to the app
  • Favorites: Your favorite channels and series preferences
  • Settings: Your app preferences (e.g., User-Agent settings)

This data is stored locally on your device using the app's internal storage and is never transmitted to any external server controlled by us.

Third-Party Services

The app connects to third-party IPTV servers based on the playlist URLs you provide. These connections are initiated by you, and the content and data handling of those servers are governed by their own privacy policies. We have no control over these third-party services.

Internet Permission

The app requires internet access solely to:

  • Load M3U/M3U8 playlists from URLs you provide
  • Stream media content from those playlists

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
